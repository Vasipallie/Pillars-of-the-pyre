PILLARS OF THE PYRE
"The world is glass. The core is fire. Five days remain."

THE STORY
The surface world remembers the day the earth bled. During a catastrophic seismic event, you are plummeted into the Nether—the ancient, volcanic foundation of existence. But the Nether is not a hell; it is a machine that has begun to break.

The Core Pillars that anchor the world are cracking, and the Fracture King, an entity born of entropy, is rising to feast on the chaos. You have five days to restore the elemental balance before the ceiling meets the floor and the world collapses into the void.

KEY FEATURES
Elemental Survival RPG: Master the four primal elements—Fire, Magma, Ash, and Obsidian—to stabilize the world.
Dynamic Environments: Experience a world that literally falls apart as you play. Watch the terrain change and hazards multiply as the 5-day clock ticks down.
Puzzle-Driven Progression: Redirect lava flows, tune obsidian frequencies, and filter toxic atmospheres to relight the Pillars.
Strategic Combat: Fight off soot-wraiths, lava sprites, and glass titans. Your resources are limited—choose your battles wisely.
Multiple Endings: Your success determines the fate of the surface. Will you achieve a Permanent Balance, a Bittersweet Survival, or witness the Total Collapse?
TECHNICAL SPECS
Genre: Pixel RPG / Survival / Puzzle
Art Style: High-contrast 2D Pixel Art
Engine: GDevelop
